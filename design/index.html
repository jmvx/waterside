Our Landscape Designer

Marybeth Harasz is committed to providing high level of service, individual attention, and quality design. She is an award winning landscape architect with twenty nine years experience working in Florida, Maryland, New Jersey, California and England. She devises customized design solutions to match the style and functional needs of her clients. Marybeth uses the traditional tools of garden design to provide timeless solutions that respond to site's specific conditions. Her designs integrate a strong design sensibility with ecological solutions adapted to the climate and ecology in which you live.

Marybeth graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Landscape Architecture Degree. She also studied garden design in Europe by completing a foreign study program in Florence, Italy as part of her landscape architecture degree. She travels extensively, studying gardens throughout the US and Europe, and uses photography as a hobby to build her idea library.

She is a California registered landscape architect and a Member of the American Institute of Certified Planners. Marybeth has received awards from the American Society of Landscape Architects, the American Planning Association and the Congress of New Urbanism.
