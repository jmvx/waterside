About Waterside

Waterside is a full service landscape company emphasizing the latest sustainable design and installation practices. Waterside works in ways that have the least impact on the environment and are in harmony with nature.

Our Clients

Our commercial clients include:

  • Royal Parks of London
  • English Nature
  • Environment Agency
  • Eton College
  • The National Trust
  • Costain
  • Mott McDonald

We have also served many residential clients in the US (Palo Alto, Campbell, and San Jose, CA) and the UK (Oxford, Wallingford, Straton-Audley, and Mixbury).

Our Services
  • Landscaping
    • Fencing, paving, turfing, ponds, planting, ground maintenance, irrigation, and tree surgery.
  • Bioengineering
    • Lakes, ponds, dredging, and river restoration
  • Design
    • Master plans, environmental mitigation, site master plans, preliminary cost estimating, and construction administration
  • Building
    • Walling, roofing, timberwork, framing, decking, and flood damage restoration
