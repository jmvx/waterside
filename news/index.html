News Updates

November/December 2015

Waterside is pleased to be given the opportunity to prepare an estate master plan for a 4-acre residence in Sonoma CA. The project is located in a rural setting and will include a front courtyard, tennis court, bocci court, swimming pool, pool house, olive orchard, farm grazing area and dog friendly landscape.

September 2015

WatersideUSA participated in an urban design study tour of Malmo, Sweden and Copenhagen, Denmark sponsored by 880 Cities. While in Europe, we also toured landscapes and gardens in the UK and Italy. (See Design)

June 2015

The design and construction of a residential garden in Bicester, UK has been completed. The idea was for something very simple that would allow the client to develop their garden further while having a usable garden for the summer. (See Photos)

April 2015

WatersideUK is pleased to have supplied staff to maintain a private river frontage in Oxfordshire, UK for a period of 6 months.
